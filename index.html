Why I Admire APJ Abdul Kalam

APJ Abdul Kalam's life and work have been a source of inspiration for millions of people. His dedication to science and technology, his humility, and his vision for a better India are just a few of the reasons why he is admired by many.

He played a pivotal role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998, which established India as a nuclear power. His contributions to the Indian space program and missile development efforts have been instrumental in making India self-reliant in defense technology.

Apart from his scientific achievements, Kalam was also a prolific writer and educator. He authored several books, including "Wings of Fire," "Ignited Minds," and "India 2020," which continue to inspire young minds across the country.

Kalam's vision for India was to transform it into a developed nation by 2020. He believed in the power of education, innovation, and self-reliance to achieve this goal. His speeches and interactions with students and young professionals have left a lasting impact on generations.
